Online Note Gallery

Why?

In universities with 50+ affiliated colleges sharing a common curriculum, many colleges lack the infrastructure to provide students with adequate study material. As a result, students must independently collect, compile, and distribute notes within their circle. Often, after exams, these notes are discarded or passed along informally, never reaching a centralized platform.

This fragmented and inefficient process wastes significant amounts of time, energy, money, and even paper (through excessive photocopying).

Thus, Online Note Gallery was born—a platform to eliminate this repetitive cycle by creating a centralized, open-access library for sharing study materials.

What?

Online Note Gallery is an open and free-for-all platform where students can:

  • Upload their study material effortlessly.
  • Download study resources shared by others.
  • Collaborate with peers and create a collective knowledge pool.

Teachers can also actively contribute by sharing their resources, notes, and guidelines, further enhancing the platform's value.

How?

Even though Online Note Gallery is an open platform, its quality is safeguarded by a moderation layer. Every contribution by a student passes through Online Note Gallery admins who review, accept, or reject submissions based on relevance and quality.

Once approved, the content becomes publicly accessible, ensuring that only useful, high-quality material is added to the library.
